Crock Pot Pulled Pork

Ingredients

4 lbs pork shoulder or butt
¼ cup tomato paste
2 cups chopped onion
½ cup apple cider vinegar
1 cup brown sugar
½ cup Tailai USA Smokey Mesquite Rub

Instructions

Combine all ingredients in crock pot and mix well. Add pork and simmer for at least 5 hours or until meat is tender and falls apart.

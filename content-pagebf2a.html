Mill Instructions


Electric Pepper Mill Instructions

TO LOAD: Hold the base firmly (the clear acrylic section) and turn the top counter clockwise. Lift the top off the base. Lift the motor section off the base and insert the six AA batteries that are provided. The minus mark (-) on the batteries should be inserted against the spring.

Fill with whole black pepper through the oblong hole in the top of the clear acrylic base. Replace the motor section on the base. Make sure that the oblong protrusion fits in the matching hole in the base and that the motor section sits flush on the base.

Replace the top over the motor section and lock it to the base by turning clockwise.

TO OPERATE: Just push the button on the top to activate the grinding mechanism and light.

HINTS: Occasionally a peppercorn may become jammed in the grinder. To break up the jammed peppercorn, press the top operating button while pushing up on the center part of the grinding mechanism.

Do not leave dead batteries in the mill.

Do not mix old and new batteries. Replace all batteries at the same time.

Do not attempt to remove grinder from mill. Please contact customer service with any questions about your mill.

 

Traditional Pepper Mill/Salt Mill Instructions

TO FILL: Remove top center finial nut. Lift off top. Pour in peppercorns/salt crystals. Replace top and finial nut.  (Do not use salt crystals in your Pepper Mill or Wet Sea Salt in your salt mill.  This may cause the grinder components to become corroded and render your mill inoperable.) 

TO ADJUST GRIND: For fine grind, turn finial nut right (clockwise). For coarser grind, turn finial nut left (counterclockwise).

TO USE MILL: Turn top to the right (clockwise).Do not turn back and forth.

TO CLEAN: Wipe off with slightly dampened cloth or paper towel. Dry thoroughly. Do not immerse in water. Do not attempt to remove grinder from mill. Please contact customer service with any questions about your mill.

 

Mess Free Pepper Mill/Salt Mill Instructions

TO FILL: Remove top center finial nut. Lift off top. Pour in peppercorns/salt crystals. Replace top and finial nut.  (Do not use salt crystals in your Pepper Mill or Wet Sea Salt in your salt mill.  This may cause the grinder components to become corroded and render your mill inoperable.) 

TO ADJUST GRIND: For fine grind, turn finial nut right (clockwise). For coarser grind, turn finial nut left (counterclockwise).

TO USE MILL: Turn top to the right (clockwise). When grinding the bottom catch will open. Do not turn back and forth.  When grinding is completed simply twist the top ¼ turn left (counter-clockwise) and the mess free catch on the bottom will close to prevent leakage.

TO CLEAN: Wipe off with slightly dampened cloth or paper towel. Dry thoroughly. Do not immerse in water. Do not attempt to remove grinder from mill. Please contact customer service with any questions about your mill.

Top Grinders Pepper Mill/Salt Mill Instructions

If the mill has a protective cap, remove cap from top of grinder.

TO ADJUST GRIND: Turn grinder adjustment knob clockwise for fine grind and counter-clockwise for coarse.

TO GRIND: Turn grinder upside down with grinding mechanism facing down.  Hold black grinder top section with one hand and turn spice compartment clockwise with other hand. Do not turn back and forth.

TO CLEAN: Wipe off with slightly dampened cloth or paper towel. Dry thoroughly. Do not immerse in water. Do not attempt to remove grinder from mill. Please contact customer service with any questions about your mill

 

 

Acrylic Combination Pepper Mill/Salt Shaker Instructions

TO FILL: Unscrew adjusting knob and salt lid. Remove top section by lifting off the shaft. Fill bottom section ¾ full of peppercorns. Fill top section ¾ full of salt. Wipe off any excess salt and slide top section back onto the shaft. Remove label from salt lid. Replace the adjusting knob.

TO ADJUST GRIND: Turn the adjusting knob to the right (clockwise) for finer grind, and left (counterclockwise) for coarser grind.

TO OPERATE MILL: Hold the bottom section and turn the top section to the right (clockwise).

TO CLEAN MILL: Simply wipe off with dampened cloth or paper towel. Dry thoroughly. Do not immerse in water. Do not attempt to remove grinder from mill. Please contact customer service with any questions about your mill.

 

Stainless Steel Combination Mill

TO FILL: Line up the top and base and merely lift off the top from the base. (pull straight up, do not turn) Once the top portion is removed, the flip tops for salt and pepper can be opened.  When putting the stainless steel top on the base make sure to have the “P” over the peppercorn and the “S” over the salt crystals

TO ADJUST GRIND:  Adjustment is found on the bottom of this mill. Turn the adjusting knob to the right (clockwise) for finer grind and left (counterclockwise) for coarser grind.

TO OPERATE MILL: Hold the bottom section and turn the top section following the arrow to P for Pepper and following the arrow to S for Salt.

TO CLEAN MILL: Simply wipe off with dampened cloth or paper towel. Dry thoroughly. Do not immerse in water. Do not attempt to remove grinder from mill. Please contact customer service with any questions about your mill.

 

Spice Grinder Directions

Remove cap from top of grinder.

TO ADJUST GRIND: Turn grinder adjustment knob clockwise for fine grind and counter-clockwise for coarse.

TO GRIND: Turn grinder upside down with grinding mechanism facing down.  Hold black grinder top section with one hand and turn spice compartment clockwise with other hand. Do not turn back and forth.

 

Pepper Pump 2190

Refiling the pepper pump is tricky but the instructions that accompany this mill are as follows:

TO FILL:: Turn the mill upside down. Loosen the adjustment wing-nut on the bottom by turning left (counterclockwise). Remove the adjustment wing-nut; fill with peppercorns.

The tricky part is keeping the shaft from turning while un-screwing the wing-nut.

I had to wedge a small tool into the space where the pepper comes out to put pressure on the shaft to loosen the adjustment wing-nut.

The wing-nut will not come off if the shaft turns as you are unscrewing the wing-nut.

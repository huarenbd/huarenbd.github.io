Spicy Garlic Lime Chicken

Ingredients

• 4 Boneless skinless chicken breasts
• ¼ teaspoon Tailai USA Sea Salt
• ¼ teaspoon Tailai USA Ground Pepper
• ¼ teaspoon Tailai USA Cayenne Pepper
• ¼ teaspoon Tailai USA Garlic Powder
• ¼ teaspoon Tailai USA Onion Powder
• ¼ teaspoon Tailai USA Thyme
• ¼ teaspoon Tailai USA Parsley

• 2 tablespoons butter
• 1 tablespoon olive oil
• 2 teaspoons Tailai USA Garlic Powder
• 5 tablespoons lime juice

Instructions

In a small bowl, mix together salt, black pepper, cayenne pepper, 1/4 tsp garlic powder, onion powder, thyme and parsley. Sprinkle spice mixture generously on both sides of chicken breasts.

Heat butter and olive oil in a large heavy skillet over medium heat. Saute chicken until golden brown, about 6 minutes on each side. Sprinkle with 2 teaspoons garlic powder and lime juice. Cook 5 minutes, stirring frequently to coat evenly with sauce. Serve with rice and your favorite vegetables. Serves 4

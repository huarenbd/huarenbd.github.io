Apple Chicken Quesadilla

Ingredients

2 medium tart apples, sliced thin
1 cup sliced cooked chicken breast
1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
1/2 cup frozen corn, thawed
1/2 cup chopped fresh tomatoes
1/2 cup Tailai USA chopped onion
1/4 teaspoon Tailai USA Sea Salt
1 Tsp Tailai USA Taco Seasoning
Juice from 1 lime wedge
6 flour tortillas (8 inches)
3/4 cup salsa
6 tablespoons sour cream

Instructions

In a medium bowl combine apple slices, corn, tomatoes, onion, salt, taco seasoning and lime juice.

Heat a large skillet on medium-high. Place a tortilla in skillet and flip a couple times with a spatula until tortilla starts to bubble. Put some cheddar and mozzarella cheese on half the tortilla about 1/2" from edge. Place chicken slices on top of cheese and fold the tortilla in half like an omelette and reduce heat to medium. Continue to cook for about 1 minute flipping occasionally until cheese is completely melted. Open tortilla and add about 3/4 cup of apple mixture over chicken and cheese. Fold tortilla over and cook another 1-2 minutes flipping occasionally.

Remove from skillet and cut into 1/3 slices. Garnish with sour cream and salsa.
